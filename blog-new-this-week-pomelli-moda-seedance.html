New This Week: Pomelli, Moda, and Seedance Join the Directory

AP
AI Pulse Checker
Mar 7, 2026 3 min read

Three new tools completed our in-depth analysis process this week and are now live in the directory. Here's a quick look at each.

Pomelli — 8.9/10

Google quietly launched Pomelli, an AI marketing tool that builds entire campaigns from your brand DNA. You feed it your brand guidelines, tone of voice, and goals, and it generates ad copy, social posts, email sequences, and landing page suggestions. The free tier is generous enough for small businesses to get real value.

Where it shines: campaign coherence. Most AI marketing tools generate isolated pieces of content. Pomelli thinks in campaigns — every piece it generates is aware of the others.

Moda — 9.3/10

Moda is the first AI design tool we've reviewed that produces fully editable output on a real canvas. Unlike Midjourney or DALL-E where the output is a flat image, Moda generates layered, editable designs you can tweak element by element. For brand teams that need on-brand assets fast, this is a genuine workflow upgrade.

Seedance — 8.6/10

ByteDance's Seedance enters the increasingly crowded AI video space. Its strength is motion quality — character movements feel more natural than Sora's current output. The pricing is competitive at the entry level, though the free tier is quite limited.
