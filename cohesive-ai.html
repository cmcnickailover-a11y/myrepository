What is Cohesive AI?

Cohesive AI is a team-oriented AI content platform with over 200 pre-built templates for everything from blog posts and social media captions to product descriptions and email campaigns. Unlike single-purpose AI writers, Cohesive provides a collaborative workspace where teams can generate, edit, refine, and approve AI content together. We tested it across 50+ content types over 40+ hours, evaluating template quality, collaboration features, and brand consistency.

The template approach distinguishes Cohesive from general-purpose chatbots. Rather than starting with a blank prompt, you select a template — LinkedIn post, product launch email, Instagram caption, SEO blog outline — and fill in contextual details. The AI then generates content specifically optimized for that format, including appropriate length, tone, and structure.

Cohesive also includes an AI editor that works like Google Docs with AI superpowers. You can highlight text and ask the AI to expand, rewrite, simplify, or change tone — making it useful for refining human-written content as well as generating new content from scratch.

Key Features & Capabilities

  • 200+ content generation templates
  • Real-time team collaboration workspace
  • Brand voice training and consistency
  • AI-powered document editor
  • Blog post and SEO content generator
  • Social media content suite
  • Multi-language content (75+ languages)
  • Content calendar and scheduling

Template quality varies significantly across the 200+ options. The best templates — blog outlines, LinkedIn posts, email subject lines, and product descriptions — generate content that is immediately usable with minor editing. The AI correctly adapts tone, length, and formatting to each content type. Social media templates are particularly strong, generating platform-appropriate content that understands character limits and engagement patterns.

Performance & Quality in Real-World Testing

The collaborative editor is Cohesive's most underrated feature. Multiple team members can work in the same document simultaneously, and the AI assistant is available to everyone in the workspace. This creates a workflow where a content strategist can generate a first draft, a copywriter can refine it with AI assistance, and a manager can approve — all in one platform.

Where It Falls Short

The weaker templates — technical writing, academic content, and long-form articles — produce generic output that requires substantial rewriting. The breadth of 200+ templates means some inevitably receive less development attention than others. We found approximately 30% of templates consistently produced subpar results.

Cohesive's AI model is not as sophisticated as ChatGPT or Claude for complex content tasks. When you push beyond template-based generation into nuanced writing, the quality gap becomes noticeable. The brand voice feature also requires significant example content to train effectively — small brands without extensive existing content will see less benefit.

Pricing & Value Analysis

⏱ Pricing verified as of March 2026 — confirm on vendor website before purchasing.

The free plan includes limited generations per month with access to all templates. The Creator plan at $25/month provides unlimited generations, brand voice training, and full collaboration features. The Team plan at $42/user/month adds team management, analytics, and priority support. Annual billing saves 20%.

At $25/month for unlimited generations, Cohesive is competitively priced against Copy.ai ($36/month) and Jasper AI ($49/month). The free tier is useful for evaluation but too restrictive for regular use. The team pricing at $42/user/month is reasonable for collaborative content workflows.

Best For

Marketing teams and content agencies who need to produce high volumes of varied content types — especially social media, email, and blog content — with team collaboration and brand consistency

Our Verdict — 7.5/10

Cohesive AI earns a 7.5/10 by providing the breadth of content templates and team collaboration that marketing teams actually need. The best templates — social media, email, and blog outlines — deliver genuine productivity gains. The weaker templates and less sophisticated AI model keep it behind the best single-purpose writers. For teams producing diverse content types at volume, Cohesive offers a practical, affordable workspace. For individual writers or those needing frontier AI quality, ChatGPT or Claude remain better choices.
