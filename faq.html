❓ AI Tools FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about AI tools, pricing, and how to choose.

Is ChatGPT free?

Yes, ChatGPT has a free tier that gives you access to GPT-4o with usage limits. The paid ChatGPT Plus plan costs $20/month and removes most limits. Read our full ChatGPT Plus review →

What's the best free AI tool?

It depends on the task. For general chat, ChatGPT's free tier is hard to beat. For research, Google's NotebookLM is completely free. For image generation, Ideogram offers a free tier. See all free AI tools →

Is Claude better than ChatGPT?

Claude Pro (9.5/10) scores higher than ChatGPT Plus (9.0/10) in our analysis, especially for long-form writing, nuance, and following complex instructions. ChatGPT wins on versatility with plugins, DALL-E, and web browsing. Full comparison →

What is an LLM?

A Large Language Model (LLM) is an AI trained on massive amounts of text that can generate, summarize, and reason about language. GPT-4, Claude, and Gemini are all LLMs. See full AI glossary →

Can AI replace my job?

AI is augmenting most jobs rather than replacing them. The tools that automate tasks (not entire jobs) are the ones seeing real adoption. Writers use AI to draft faster, developers use it to debug, marketers use it to generate variations. The people most at risk are those who refuse to learn AI tools, not those who embrace them.

Are AI-generated images copyrightable?

This is an evolving legal area. In the US, purely AI-generated images without significant human creative input are generally not copyrightable. However, images where a human made substantial creative choices in prompting and post-processing may qualify. Always check the terms of service for the specific tool you use.

What's the difference between GPT-4 and GPT-5?

GPT-5 (released late 2025) is significantly better at reasoning, following instructions, and multimodal tasks compared to GPT-4. It powers ChatGPT Plus and offers improved coding, analysis, and creative output. Read our ChatGPT Plus review →

Is Midjourney worth it?

At $10/month for the Basic plan, Midjourney offers the best artistic quality in AI image generation. If you need images for creative projects, it's worth it. For product photos or realistic renders, consider alternatives. Full Midjourney review →

What is vibe coding?

Vibe coding is a 2025/2026 trend where you describe what you want in plain English and AI generates working code. Tools like Cursor, Lovable, and Bolt.new are leading this movement. Read our analysis →

What does 'AI hallucination' mean?

An AI hallucination is when a model generates something that sounds plausible but is factually wrong. All current AI models can hallucinate, which is why fact-checking AI output is important. See full glossary →
