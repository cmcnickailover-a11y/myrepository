What is SlidesAI?

SlidesAI is a Google Slides add-on that uses AI to generate complete presentations from text input. Paste your notes, outline, or full text, choose a visual style, and SlidesAI generates a multi-slide presentation with appropriate layouts, images, and formatting — all within Google Slides. We analyzed it by generating 15 presentations across business pitches, educational lectures, and team updates over in-depth analysis.

The key differentiator is workflow integration. While tools like Gamma and Tome create presentations in their own platforms, SlidesAI generates directly inside Google Slides — meaning your presentations are immediately shareable, collaboratively editable, and stored in your existing Google Drive. For organizations already standardized on Google Workspace, this eliminates the friction of adopting another platform.

SlidesAI supports multiple generation modes: from text (paste your content), from topic (AI generates content and slides), and from URL (summarize a webpage into slides). Each mode serves a different workflow — converting existing content, creating from scratch, or summarizing research.

Key Features & Capabilities

  • Google Slides native add-on
  • Text-to-presentation generation
  • Topic-based deck creation
  • URL-to-slides conversion
  • Multiple design themes
  • Automatic image selection
  • Speaker notes generation
  • Multi-language support (100+ languages)

From-text generation is SlidesAI's strongest mode. Pasting a well-structured outline or document produced presentations that captured the key points accurately and distributed content across slides logically. The AI correctly identified section breaks, created appropriate headers, and selected relevant stock images. For converting meeting notes into shareable presentations or turning reports into slide decks, it saves significant manual work.

The Google Slides integration is seamless. Generated presentations behave exactly like any other Google Slides file — you can edit individual slides, collaborate with team members, present directly, and export to PowerPoint. There is no export step, no compatibility issues, and no learning curve beyond the initial add-on installation.

Where It Falls Short

Design quality is SlidesAI's primary limitation. Generated slides look competent but generic — they follow safe layouts with predictable image placement and standard typography. Compared to Gamma's visually striking output or Canva's design variety, SlidesAI presentations are functional rather than impressive. For client-facing pitches or public presentations, manual design refinement is essential.

The free plan is restrictive — limited to 3 presentations per month with basic themes. This is barely enough to evaluate the tool, let alone use it regularly. The topic-based generation mode also produces shallow content that requires significant editing, making it less useful than the text-based mode.

Pricing & Value Analysis

⏱ Pricing verified as of March 2026 — confirm on vendor website before purchasing.

The free plan includes 3 presentations per month with basic themes. The Basic plan at $10/month provides unlimited presentations, premium themes, and AI image selection. The Pro plan at $20/month adds priority processing, advanced customization, and URL-to-slides. Annual billing offers 2 months free.

At $10/month for unlimited presentations, SlidesAI is among the most affordable AI presentation tools. Compared to Gamma ($8/month with export limitations) or Beautiful.ai ($12/month with its own editor), SlidesAI's Google Slides integration justifies the price for Google Workspace teams.
